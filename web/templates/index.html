About

Su Chef

Su Chef answers your cooking questions and builds recipes around real nutrition goals, by voice or text, while you're still at the stove.

It pairs a conversational chef with a large food dataset and a trained nutrition model, so any recipe can be searched, generated, and measured against the way you want to eat. Su Chef is an experimental student project; its nutrition figures are estimates, not medical advice.

model_trainingHow the nutrition model works

Su Chef learned from about 38,000 real recipes that each carry measured nutrition. From those it trained a model that estimates a recipe's per-serving calories, protein, carbs, and fat from its ingredients. Searched “Chef recipes” show their real, measured numbers; for recipes Su Chef generates, the model double-checks the nutrition. You can try the estimator yourself in Recipe Lab → Estimate a meal.
