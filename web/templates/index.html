The data & model behind it

model_trainingHow the nutrition model works

The app learned from about 38,000 real recipes that each carry measured nutrition. From those it trained a model that estimates a recipe's per-serving calories, protein, carbs, and fat from its ingredients. Searched “Chef recipes” show their real, measured numbers; for generated recipes, the model double-checks the nutrition. Try it yourself in Recipe Lab → Calculate Nutrition.

Meet the agents

A kitchen crew reviews every recipe you get, and a data crew (a CrewAI pipeline) built the nutrition model behind the app. Each is a team of focused agents.

A course project. Su Chef is an experimental project built for the AI Development & Collaboration course. It simulates how an AI product team turns raw data into a working, predictive product.

Ethics & safety. Nutrition figures are model estimates, not medical or dietary advice. AI answers and recipes can be wrong, so use your judgment, especially around allergies and food safety.
